Scorpions

Scorpions are arachnids known for their venomous sting and glow under ultraviolet light.

Interesting Fact

  • They glow in the dark under ultraviolet light due to substances in their exoskeleton.

Scorpions

Scorpions are ancient and dangerous creatures. Here are some facts:

  • Scorpions have existed for over 400 million years.
  • They can survive extreme conditions, including high radiation levels.
  • There are about 2,000 species of scorpions, but only around 30 have venom deadly to humans.
  • Scorpions can slow their metabolism to survive without food for up to a year.
  • Scorpions give birth to live young, unlike most arachnids.
  • Their venom is primarily used to paralyze prey, not for defense.
  • They use their pincers to crush prey before stinging.
  • Scorpions are nocturnal hunters.
  • The emperor scorpion is one of the largest species, growing up to 20 cm in length.
