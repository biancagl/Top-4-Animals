Mosquitoes

Mosquitoes are one of the deadliest animals, responsible for spreading diseases such as malaria, dengue, and Zika.

Mosquitoes

Did you know that mosquitoes are considered the deadliest animals on Earth? Here are some interesting facts:

  • They have been around for over 100 million years.
  • There are more than 3,000 species of mosquitoes worldwide.
  • Only female mosquitoes bite humans.
  • A mosquito can drink up to three times its body weight in blood.
  • They are attracted to carbon dioxide exhaled by humans.
  • Their bites cause itching because of the proteins in their saliva.
  • They can detect body heat and sweat from a distance.
  • Mosquitoes have a lifespan of about 2-4 weeks.
  • They are most active at dawn and dusk.
